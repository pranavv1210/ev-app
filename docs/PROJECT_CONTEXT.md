# PROJECT CONTEXT

## Product Vision
Yaatra EV is a Bengaluru-first EV-only ride-hailing platform that aims to revolutionize urban mobility by prioritizing sustainability, premium ride experiences, and safety. Inspired by Namma Yatri, Uber, and BluSmart.

## User Types
- **Customers**: Everyday riders seeking reliable, clean, and safe transportation.
- **Drivers**: Verified partners driving EV Bikes, EV Autos, or EV Cabs.
- **Admins**: Platform operators managing the fleet, drivers, users, and overall system health.

## Platforms
- Customer App
- Driver App
- Admin Dashboard

## Features
- EV-only rides
- Women Mode (matching female passengers with female drivers)
- Carbon footprint tracking
- Premium ride experience

## Launch City
- Bengaluru

## Core Differentiators
- 100% Electric Vehicle fleet (No exceptions, no compromise)
- Women Mode for enhanced social safety
- Built-in carbon footprint tracking
- Premium, silent, and comfortable ride experience
- Specifically tailored for the Bengaluru market

## Business Goals
- Drive high EV adoption in urban transit.
- Repair the city's air quality kilometer by kilometer.
- Establish a dominant presence in Bengaluru's mobility sector.

## Product Philosophy
Sustainability as the standard, not an option. Premium travel that repairs the environment without compromising on comfort or safety.
