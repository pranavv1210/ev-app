# VoltRide Design System & Landing Page Specifications

## Global Theme & Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: Apple / Tesla / Linear / Stripe / BluSmart fusion. Futuristic, premium, sophisticated, clean, trustworthy, and environmentally conscious.
- **Theme**: Dark Mode Default.
- **Background**: Near-black charcoal (`#0F1115` or similar deep grey).
- **Text/Typography**: High contrast white (`#FFFFFF`) for primary text. Light grey (`#A1A1AA`) for secondary text. Typography must be modern (e.g., Inter, SF Pro, or Outfit) with large, bold headlines and elegant spacing.
- **Colors**:
  - Primary Accent: Electric Green (`#00FF66`).
  - Secondary Accent: Premium Emerald (`#059669`).
  - Success: Soft Green (`#34D399`).
  - Warning: Amber (`#FBBF24`).
- **Components Style**: Glassmorphism with deep blurs, subtle soft shadows, rounded corners (e.g., 16px to 24px), layered depth effects.
- **Motion**: Scroll-based storytelling, parallax, layered depth, smooth reveal animations, staggered appearances, hover transformations. No gimmicky or aggressive animations.

## Component Hierarchy

### Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: Electric Green background, bold black text, soft glow effect. Hover: Slight scale up and brightness increase.
- **Secondary CTA**: Transparent with subtle grey/white border or glassmorphic background, white text. Hover: Border color transitions to Electric Green.

### Cards
- **Feature/Vehicle Cards**: Floating glassmorphic containers. Charcoal background with slight transparency, 1px subtle white/grey top-border to simulate light reflection. Soft shadow underneath.
